Chicken Biryani


A flavorful Indian rice dish made with marinated chicken and aromatic spices

Recipe:

marinate the chicken
In a large bowl, combine all the marinade ingredients.
Mix well to coat the chicken pieces. Cover and
refrigerate for at least 1 hour, or overnight for
best flavor.

Prepare the Rice
Wash the basmati rice under cold running water
until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for
30 minutes, then drain.

Cook the rice
In a large pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil.
Add the soaked and drained rice along with the
cardamom pods, cloves, cinnamon stick, and salt.
Cook the rice until it is 70% cooked.
Drain the rice and set aside.

Cook the onions
Heat the ghee or oil in a separate large pot.
Add the sliced onions and cook until golden brown.

Add the seeds
Add the cumin seeds and fennel seeds to the pot.
Cook until they start to sizzle.

Add ginger-garlic paste
Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute
until fragrant.

Add the Spices
Add the chopped tomatoes, green chilies,
coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder,
and garam masala. Cook until the tomatoes are soft
and oil starts to separate.

Cook the Chicken
Add the marinated chicken and cook until it is
browned on all sides.

Layer the Rice
Layer half of the partially cooked rice over the chicken
in the pot. Sprinkle half of the chopped cilantro
and mint leaves on top.

Continue Layering
Repeat the layering process with the remaining
rice and herbs.

Cook the Biryani
Cover the pot tightly with a lid and cook on low heat for
20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through
and the rice is fully cooked and aromatic.

Garnish and Serve
Gently fluff the rice with a fork and mix everything together.
Garnish with fried onions, toasted cashews,raisins, and
fresh cilantro leaves.
